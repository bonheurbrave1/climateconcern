Our Audit Approach

Climate Concern provides independent local audit services to verify the environmental integrity, social benefits, and financial transparency of climate projects. Our audit team combines international standards with local expertise to deliver credible verification services.

As Rwanda's leading independent auditor for climate projects, we ensure that initiatives deliver real, measurable, and additional climate benefits while complying with national and international standards.

Our Audit Process

A structured approach to independent verification

1

Engagement & Planning

We begin with understanding your project, objectives, and specific verification needs to develop a customized audit plan.

2

Documentation Review

Thorough examination of project documentation, methodologies, monitoring plans, and data management systems.

3

Field Assessment

On-site visits to verify implementation, interview stakeholders, and validate project activities and outcomes.

4

Data Analysis

Rigorous analysis of project data, calculations, and methodologies to ensure accuracy and compliance with standards.

5

Reporting

Comprehensive audit report detailing findings, verification statements, and recommendations for improvement.

6

Certification & Follow-up

Issuance of verification certificates and ongoing support for maintaining compliance and addressing findings.
