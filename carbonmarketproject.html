Our Development Approach

Climate Concern specializes in developing high-quality carbon credit projects that deliver real environmental impact while generating sustainable revenue streams. Our comprehensive approach covers every stage from initial feasibility to market-ready carbon credits.

With deep expertise in Rwanda's climate landscape and international carbon markets, we help organizations transform their sustainability initiatives into verified carbon assets that contribute to global climate goals.

Our Development Process

A structured pathway from concept to carbon credits

1

Project Scoping

Initial assessment of project concept, eligibility screening, and preliminary feasibility analysis to determine carbon credit potential.

2

Methodology Selection

Identification and selection of appropriate carbon credit methodologies based on project type and requirements.

3

Baseline Development

Establishment of project baselines, additionality demonstration, and emission reduction quantification.

4

Documentation

Preparation of comprehensive project design documents and supporting materials for validation.

5

Stakeholder Consultation

Community engagement, stakeholder consultation, and social impact assessment as required by standards.

6

Validation & Registration

Coordination with third-party validators and support through the project registration process.

7

Implementation Support

Ongoing support during project implementation, monitoring, and reporting phases.

8

Credit Issuance

Support through verification and carbon credit issuance processes for ongoing revenue generation.
