About ClimateConcern

ClimateConcern is Rwanda's leading climate action organization, dedicated to supporting sustainable development through innovative environmental solutions. We specialize in carbon market development, policy advisory services, and capacity building initiatives.

Our team of experts works closely with government agencies, private sector organizations, and communities to implement effective climate strategies that align with Rwanda's development goals.
