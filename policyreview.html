Policy Review Services

Expert analysis and assessment of climate policies for effective implementation

Our Policy Review Approach

Climate Concern provides comprehensive policy review services to assess the effectiveness, feasibility, and alignment of climate policies with national and international objectives. Our expert team analyzes existing policies and provides actionable recommendations for improvement.

As Rwanda's leading climate policy advisory firm, we ensure that policies are evidence-based, stakeholder-inclusive, and designed for successful implementation across all sectors.

Our Policy Review Process

A systematic approach to comprehensive policy analysis

1

Scoping & Objectives

Define review scope, objectives, and methodology in consultation with stakeholders to ensure comprehensive coverage.

2

Document Analysis

Systematic review of policy documents, implementation reports, legal frameworks, and supporting materials.

3

Stakeholder Consultation

Engage with government agencies, civil society, private sector, and communities to gather diverse perspectives.

4

Data Collection & Analysis

Gather implementation data, monitor indicators, and analyze policy performance against stated objectives.

5

Assessment & Evaluation

Evaluate policy effectiveness, efficiency, relevance, and sustainability using established criteria and frameworks.

6

Recommendations & Reporting

Develop actionable recommendations and comprehensive report with findings, conclusions, and implementation roadmap.
