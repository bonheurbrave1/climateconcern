Our Verification Approach

Climate Concern provides comprehensive verification services for operational carbon credit projects, ensuring continued compliance with international standards and facilitating regular carbon credit issuance.

Our experienced verification team conducts thorough assessments of project performance, monitoring data, and emission reductions to maintain the integrity and credibility of your carbon credits in the marketplace.

Verification Cycle

Understanding the ongoing verification timeline

Typical Verification Schedule

Annual Verification

Most projects require annual verification for carbon credit issuance, typically scheduled 12 months after project registration.

Verification Timeline

Each verification cycle typically takes 6-8 weeks from initiation to final report submission to the registry.

Credit Issuance

Following successful verification, carbon credits are typically issued within 30-45 days by the registry.

Our Verification Process

A systematic approach to carbon credit verification

1

Pre-Verification Planning

Review monitoring reports, project documentation, and plan verification activities including site visit schedules and stakeholder interviews.

2

Desk Review

Comprehensive desk-based review of monitoring reports, calculations, data management systems, and compliance with methodology requirements.

3

Site Verification

On-site verification activities including physical inspections, equipment checks, stakeholder interviews, and data validation.

4

Finding Resolution

Communication of verification findings, coordination with project proponents for clarifications and corrective actions as needed.

5

Data Analysis

Detailed analysis of emission reduction calculations, verification of baseline assumptions, and assessment of additionality.

6

Verification Report

Preparation of comprehensive verification report documenting findings, verified emission reductions, and compliance status.

7

Registry Submission

Submission of verification documents to the appropriate registry and support through the credit issuance process.

8

Follow-up Support

Ongoing support for addressing registry queries and preparation for subsequent verification cycles.
