Meet the visionary leaders driving EnviroServe's mission for a sustainable future

Our Leadership Team

Experienced professionals committed to environmental sustainability

Jean Ntazinda

Lead Consultant, Climate Finance

With over 15 years of experience, Mr. Ntazinda is a carbon market expert who has designed projects generating millions of carbon credits. He now focuses on implementing the Paris Agreement and supporting climate initiatives across Africa

Ms. Mary Alice Bamusiime

Lead Consultant, Gender & Environmental Safeguards

Ms. Alice Bamusiime has over 20 years of experience in gender mainstreaming across natural resources, agriculture, renewable energy, and policy development. She has worked with USAID programs and now consults widely on gender, environment, and rural development in Rwanda and beyond.

Peter Katanisa

Economist (Environment and Natural Resource Management Specialist).

Peter Katanisa is a seasoned development professional with over 15 years of experience in climate change, natural resources, and conservation. He currently leads the Africa Natural Capital Accounting Community of Practice for the World Bank and advises on major climate finance initiatives

Omer ELAWAD

Lead Consultant, Carbon Market

Omer is a climate finance and carbon markets specialist with over 18 years of experience advancing environmental sustainability across Africa. He has worked with institutions such as the World Bank and REMA to operationalize Rwanda’s Standardized Crediting Framework and support Article 6 implementation, while also contributing to national, regional, and voluntary carbon market initiatives across multiple countries.

Dominique Kayigire

Lead Consultant, Environmental Compliance

Dominique, an MSc graduate in Geo-Science for Environment and Sustainable Development, co-designed EnviroFlag under Climate Concern Ltd. With nine years in natural resources governance, he has led roles including Rwanda Country Manager at RCS Global Group and consultant for the former Ministry of Natural Resources

Dieudonné UMUGISHA

Managing Consultant

Umugisha Dieudonne, with over 10 years’ experience in research operations and project management, has led projects in climate action, clean energy, agriculture, health, and youth employment. Holding degrees in Electrical Engineering and Renewable Energy, he is PRINCE2 certified with expertise in climate finance, carbon taxation, and sustainability
