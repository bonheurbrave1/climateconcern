About Climate Concern

Leading climate action in Rwanda since 2012

Our Story

Founded in 2012, Climate Concern has grown to become Rwanda's leading consulting and advisory firm specializing in environment and climate change solutions. Our journey began with a simple vision: to help organizations navigate the complex landscape of climate regulations while promoting sustainable development.

Over the years, we have built a reputation for excellence in legal and regulatory compliance, GHG emissions reduction, and climate change adaptation projects. Our team of experts works across various sectors to deliver practical, innovative solutions that address the pressing challenges of climate change.

Today, we stand as a trusted partner to government agencies, private sector organizations, and communities across Rwanda and the East African region.
